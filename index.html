2048-Game

The 2048 game is a tile-based puzzle where the player uses arrow keys to slide numbered tiles on a 4x4 grid. When two tiles with the same number collide, they merge into one, doubling the value. The objective is to continue merging tiles to reach the number 2048. After every move, a new tile (either 2 or 4) appears in a random empty spot, and the game ends when no more moves are possible. The challenge lies in strategically combining tiles while managing limited space.
